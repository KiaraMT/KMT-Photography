Fire in the Sky

I will have to say I got pretty lucky with this photograph. The lighting was a bit off but I was thankful that I got to take this photo. With phone cameras, taking photos of the sun it can be difficult because the exposure of the sun's rays can be hard to capture the sunset. So it is best to play around with different angles, and let the camera fully adjust to the lighting.
