A little About Me



Hello future photographers in the making! I'm Kiara Trusty. I am a student at Johnson & Wales University. I have always dreamed about being a photographer, and I really considered it as a carrier in the future. It's hard for students to buy a professional camera, but thankfully phone cameras can take great photos, don't let anyone tell you different. I unfornately don't have a professional camera of my own. However, I believe that phone cameras can take amazing photography as well at a low cost. Here I will be featuring some of my own photography, and also sharing some tips and tricks that I've learned along the way using a phone camera. I will post of my photographs and give a little description of each photo. Thanks for visting my blog And I will continue to post may artwork to keep all of you guys updated.
