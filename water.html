August 16, 2015 Block Island, RI

Don't Look Down

This shot would have to be my favorite photo that I took while I was visiting Block Island. It's just a simple shot of a cliff looking down to the water. The cliff was so high, but the view of the beaches was amazing. Lighting played a huge role in order to get this shot. I simply wanted to capture the sun shine on the water.
